18 The Subscriber {#DCPS_Modules_Subscriber}
19 ===============
20 
21 The Subscriber is responsible for collecting data from various publications:
22 
23 - Each datatype must be accessed using its own typed \ref DCPS_Modules_Subscription_DataReader "DataReader".
24 - A \ref DCPS_Modules_Subscription_DataReader "DataReader" gives access to samples and their corresponding \ref DCPS_Modules_Subscription_SampleInfo "SampleInfo".
25 - SampleInfo describes relevant information about the state of a sample and the instance to which it relates, for example about its lifecycle.
26 - The Subscriber acts as a factory for typed DataReaders.
27 - DataReaders do not read their data directly from the network:
28  - it is the Subscriber that decides when and how the data is transmitted to its DataReaders:
29 - Groups of messages (possibly coming from different DataWriters) can be received as a whole, allowing for a sort of transaction. (See \ref DCPS_QoS_Presentation "PresentationQoSPolicy").
30 - A Subscriber only collects samples that are published in the selected Partitions (See \ref DCPS_QoS_Partition PartitionQosPolicy.)
